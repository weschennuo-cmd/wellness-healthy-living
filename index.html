Content Framework

Strategy

Position POCOCO as part of a balanced lifestyle, not just a sleep product.

Connect it naturally with wellness habits, recovery routines, fitness, nutrition, travel or daily self-care.

Reference

Possible content directions include:

  • Daily wellness routines
  • Recovery after workouts
  • Healthy evening habits
  • High-performance lifestyle
  • Travel recovery
  • Morning vs evening routines
  • Podcasts or educational discussions about wellness
Investment

Share how your environment supports your overall wellbeing.

Show that wellness is built through many small habits working together.

Inspiration

Connect POCOCO naturally with your lifestyle instead of making it the main subject.

Performance

Lifestyle storytelling usually performs better than feature-focused content.
